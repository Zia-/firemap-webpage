Active Fire Products

Fire Detection and Monitoring
Summary

Satellite instruments can either detect the thermal emission during a fire or the burnt area after. The latter are known as "burnt area", "burnt scar", "burnt pixel" or "fire affected area", while the first products are referred to as "hot spots", "active fires", "fire pixels" or "fire counts" (Kaiser et al., 2009http://link.aip.org/link/?APCPCS/1100/645/1).
